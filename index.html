What is Reliance MET City?
+

Reliance MET City (Model Economic Township) is a large-scale integrated township by Reliance Industries Limited in Jhajjar, Haryana, near Gurugram. Spread across 8,250+ acres, it combines industrial, residential, and commercial spaces with modern infrastructure and a walk-to-work concept.

What does MET stand for?
+

MET stands for Model Economic Township.

What is the master plan of Reliance MET City?
+

The master plan includes:

  • Industrial zones (including Japan Industrial Township)
  • Residential communities with green spaces
  • Commercial hubs and shopping areas
  • Wide roads, power supply, water treatment, sewerage & telecom networks
  • Sustainable initiatives like renewable energy and waste management
What is the Ambani Jhajjar project?
+

The Ambani-linked project in Jhajjar is Reliance MET City, developed by Reliance Industries Limited to create a modern economic hub with industrial, commercial, and residential development.

Is Reliance MET City a good investment?
+

Yes. Due to its strategic NCR location, large-scale infrastructure, industrial presence, and strong development backing by Reliance Industries, MET City offers strong long-term growth and investment potential.
